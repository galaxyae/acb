Order Management

It’s often easiest to fulfill and manage your orders when you’re just starting out in the e-commerce world. But when your business starts growing, things can get a bit complicated.

Managing a huge volume of orders requires a streamlined order management system. A Customs Brokerage is an expert in managing e-commerce orders from the start of their journey to the point when they reach your customers.

Order Management Services We Offer

Our order management services include everything from receiving your orders to packaging and shipping them to end consumers. We have a team of experts who can streamline the entire process through multiple channels to ensure your customers get the right products on time.

We use state-of-the-art order management software (OMS) that updates every step of the process in real-time, ensuring everyone is on the same page.

As soon as we receive an order, we send it to our order fulfillment center. Our warehouse logistics teams pick the right products from the inventory and package them for safe transportation. Our OMS automatically sends the order’s tracking number to the customer, enabling them to monitor its status until it reaches their front door.

Benefits of Our Order Management Services

By utilizing our order management services, you’ll avoid order fulfillment mistakes, which happen quite often in e-commerce. Our experienced logistics teams will ensure orders are accurate and reach your customers within the specified time. Thanks to our automated process, there’s no room for human error.

Working with us also means preventing overstocking and understocking. Since we have full, real-time visibility into your entire inventory, we can help you avoid stocking too much or too little of your merchandise.

If you understock, you risk not being able to keep up with the demand. You risk irritating your customers and losing sales, as they may end up purchasing from your competition. We’re here to help you prevent that.
