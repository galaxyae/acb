The Future of Logistics: ACBInsight

ACBInsight is our cutting-edge shipping platform, created to elevate your concierge experience. The dashboard provides you with live step-by-step updates on your containers and shipments, a secure location to upload and store your pertinent documents, and a direct chat to a Concierge Agent that will assist you on any particular shipment. The platform pushes a level of visibility not seen anywhere else in the industry, as it tracks your shipment from POL, on it’s trip, through customs, to its POD, and to its final destination, all while simultaneously showing you ACB’s side of the responsibilities.

Purpose

A Customs Brokerage was founded with one purpose: To Serve. As men and women of service, we push ourselves to provide you with the top concierge experience. To evolve this experience, our team built, from scratch, our very own shipping dashboard: ACBInsight. This next stage of our concierge service combines the last 46 years of ACB to bring you bigger and better Peace of Mind. That is why this platform is free of cost to our clients, as its created for you.

 

Optimize your logistics

Live Shipment Updates

Direct updates from Open Track let you know exactly where your shipment is and its status, whenever and wherever. With notifications and precise filtering, all your shipments are easy to keep your eye on. Never worry about your shipments again with ACBinsight on your side.

 

Efficient Document Upload

Every document you need to upload can be done straight on your computer, without a long thread of emails to clutter your inbox along with it. ACBinsight processes these straight into our system, greatly improving efficiency. It also doubles as a storage location, keeping records of all important document safe and in one place for your convenience. Make your life easier with ACBInsight.

 

Direct Concierge Chat

Each shipment has the option to open up a chat. No question is too big or small for our Concierges, so if you have a concern, get your answer right away through our direct chat system, connecting you to a real, live team member at A Customs Brokerage.
