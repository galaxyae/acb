ACB is Bridging oceans, bringing water with

Water For All

impacting the world one shipment at a time

A Customs Brokerage has pledged to donate a percentage of each shipment to bring clean water to communities with BLUE.

Today 1 in 10 people lack access to safe drinking water, but each year, more people globally face water scarcity due to pollution, deforestation, population growth, misuse, and changing climate patterns. 

A Customs Brokerage has a deep history with BLUE, with some of our founding members attending mission trips throughout the years. Now, with Water For All, we can use our business to positively impact these communities at a larger scale, unlocking them from cycles of poverty, sickness, and low education levels that lack of clean water inflicts.

It is part of BLUE’s message that anyone can make an impact and that everyone deserves the opportunity to make their own, and we want to continue to extend that.

the effect

BLUE advocates for a world where there is water for all, forever. Through service projects and sustainability education, BLUE connects families in Latin America to clean, fresh water and inspires young people to lead the way to a water-secure future.

That is a mission that aligns with us at A Customs Brokerage as people who live To Serve. We want to contribute to their mission in any way we can! BLUE has helped many communities so far and we not only want to help, but we want to call on others to join in to keep deepening the impact.

join the cause

As one entity, our contribution will help many, but if we continue to band together, we can change the face of the planet. Everyone deserves access to clean water, join us in helping BLUE achieve that mission.
